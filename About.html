About Us

Who We Are

The Bridge Foundation is committed to empowering women and youths through innovation, renewable energy solutions, health advocacy, entrepreneurship, and leadership development.

Vision

Empowering a generation of vibrant and inclusive communities where youths and women thrive as catalysts for transformative change.

Mission

To bridge gaps in capacity, opportunity, and social justice through education, advocacy, and community-driven initiatives.

Core Values

  • Empowerment
  • Innovation
  • Leadership
  • Equity & Inclusion
  • Collaboration

Problems We Solve

Gender Gap in Education

Over 20 million Nigerian children are out of school, with girls making up nearly 60%.

Renewable Energy

85 million Nigerians lack reliable electricity, affecting women-led households and rural businesses.

Health Disparities

Nigeria has one of the highest maternal mortality rates globally.

Leadership Representation

Women hold less than 10% of leadership positions in Nigeria.

Youth Unemployment

Over 53% of Nigerian youths are unemployed.
